born and raised in north london, jessi clover belgrave is a director of caribbean, english and danish heritage whose visual language is shaped by a music-led instinct.

she began in music as a singer, songwriter and producer, creating for her own artist projects as well as major brands and hollywood films before moving into directing.

as one half of plastic fruit studios, she works across music videos, advertising, editorial and photography.
